Privacy Policy

Effective date: December 2025

PocketPal ("we", "us", or "our") provides a software platform that helps students and institutions plan, study, and collaborate. This Privacy Notice explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you use our websites, applications, and services (the "Services").

1. Scope

This Privacy Notice applies to all PocketPal web and mobile properties, admin portals, and APIs. When we provide the Services to or through an educational institution, we process personal information on the institution's behalf as a service provider/processor. Where an institution's policy conflicts, the institution's agreement controls.

1.1 Applicable Services

  • • PocketPal web application
  • • Mobile applications (if applicable)
  • • Admin portals
  • • Application Programming Interfaces (APIs)
  • • All associated services and features

2. Information We Collect

PocketPal collects the following categories of personal information:

2.1 Account & Contact Information

  • • Full name
  • • Email address
  • • Institution affiliation
  • • User role (student, administrator, etc.)
  • • Authentication identifiers
  • • Unique user identifiers

2.2 Educational/Academic Data

  • • Course enrollments and schedules
  • • Academic year and major
  • • Study group participation
  • • In-app messages and communications
  • • Poll responses and voting data
  • • Academic preferences and study habits

Note: We treat educational/academic data as confidential institutional data and handle it with additional safeguards.

2.3 Usage & Log Data

  • • Pages viewed and navigation patterns
  • • Timestamps of activities
  • • System diagnostics
  • • Error logs and crash reports
  • • Feature usage statistics

2.4 Device/Network Data

  • • IP addresses
  • • Device type and operating system
  • • Browser information
  • • Network information
  • • Connection metadata

Purpose: Collected for security, fraud prevention, and abuse prevention.

2.5 Feedback & Support

  • • Bug reports
  • • Support ticket submissions
  • • Survey responses
  • • Email communications
  • • Feature requests

2.6 Cookies & Similar Technologies

  • • Session management cookies
  • • Security tokens
  • • User preference settings
  • • Load balancing identifiers

Note: We use strictly necessary cookies for essential operations. We do not currently use analytics or advertising cookies, but if we add them in the future, we will update this Notice and request consent where required.

3. Sources of Information

We collect personal information from the following sources:

  • • Directly from Users: Information provided during account creation, profile setup, and use of Services.
  • • From Service Usage: Automatically collected data from user interactions with the Services.
  • • From Educational Institutions: Data provided by institutions to enable Services (e.g., course rosters, student information).
  • • From Partners: Information from third-party service providers necessary for Service operation.

4. How We Use Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • • To provide, operate, maintain, and secure the Services.
  • • To facilitate features such as academic planning, study-group matching, messaging, and polling.
  • • To maintain and improve performance, fix bugs, and develop new features.
  • • To provide support and important Service communications.
  • • To comply with law, enforce terms, and protect the rights, safety, and security of users and institutions.

Important Restrictions:

  • • We do not sell personal information.
  • • We do not use student data for targeted advertising.

5. Legal Bases for Processing (EEA/UK)

Where GDPR/UK GDPR applies, we process personal data based on the following legal bases: to perform our contract with you or your institution; for our legitimate interests in providing and improving secure Services; to comply with legal obligations; and with consent where required.

6. How We Share Information

We share personal information only in the following circumstances:

  • • Service Providers: With providers under contract for hosting, storage, authentication, email, support, and security monitoring (e.g., AWS, Supabase).
  • • Institutional Administrators: When deployed for an institution, consistent with institutional policies and agreements.
  • • Legal Requirements: Where required by law or to protect users, institutions, or our Services.
  • • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or reorganization.

We do not "sell" or "share" personal information as defined by CCPA/CPRA. We do not share information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

7. International Transfers

We may transfer personal information to countries where our service providers operate, including the United States. Where required by law, we implement appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs).

8. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use strictly necessary cookies for login session management, security, load balancing, and user preferences. If we add analytics or advertising cookies in the future, we will update this Notice and, where required, request your consent.

9. Security Measures

We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal information, including encryption in transit and at rest, role-based access controls, multi-factor authentication for administrative systems, and continuous security monitoring. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure; while we implement industry-standard security measures, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Data Retention

We retain personal information as long as needed to provide the Services and meet legal/contractual obligations. Institutional data is returned and deleted pursuant to contract terms; upon termination or verified request, we delete hosted copies within 30 days unless law requires longer retention.

11. Your Rights & Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • • The right to access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • • The right to data portability.
  • • The right to opt-out of "sale/sharing" (not applicable as we do not sell or share personal information).
  • • The right to limit the use of sensitive information (not applicable as we do not use it in ways requiring this right).

If we deny a request, you may appeal by replying to our decision email. To submit a request, please email us at pocketpalrevolution@gmail.com. If we process your information on behalf of an institution, we may direct your request to that institution.

12. Children's Privacy

The Services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information, contact us immediately and we will take appropriate steps to delete the information.

13. Changes to This Notice

We may update this Notice from time to time. We will post updates with a new "Effective date." If changes materially affect your rights, we will notify you and your institution as required. Continued use of the Services after changes constitutes acceptance.

14. Contact Us

For questions about this Notice or our privacy practices, please email: pocketpalrevolution@gmail.com.
