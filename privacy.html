Privacy Notice

Effective date: December 2025

PocketPal ("we", "us", or "our") provides software that helps students and institutions plan, study, and collaborate. This Privacy Notice explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you use our websites, applications, and services (the "Services").

1. Scope

This Notice applies to all PocketPal web and mobile properties, admin portals, and APIs. When we provide the Services to or through an educational institution, we process personal information on the institution's behalf as a service provider/processor. Where an institution's policy conflicts, the institution's agreement controls.

2. Information We Collect

  • - Account & Contact Information (name, email, institution/role, authentication identifiers).
  • - Educational/Academic Data provided by you or your institution (courses, schedules, study‑group participation, messages or polls in‑app). We treat this as confidential institutional data.
  • - Usage & Log Data (pages viewed, timestamps, diagnostics, error logs).
  • - Device/Network Data gathered for security, fraud, and abuse prevention.
  • - Feedback & Support (bug reports, surveys, emails).
  • - Cookies & Similar Technologies used for essential operations (session management, security).

3. Sources of Information

We collect information directly from you, from your use of the Services, and from institutions or partners who provide information to enable the Services.

4. How We Use Information

  • Provide, operate, and secure the Services (authentication, authorization, fraud prevention, incident response).
  • Facilitate features such as academic planning, study‑group matching, messaging, polling, and analytics you choose to use.
  • Maintain and improve performance, fix bugs, develop new features.
  • Provide support and important Service communications.
  • Comply with law, enforce terms, and protect the rights, safety, and security of users and institutions.
  • We do not sell personal information and we do not use student data for targeted advertising.

5. Legal Bases (EEA/UK only)

Where GDPR/UK GDPR applies, we process personal data to perform a contract (with you or your institution), for our legitimate interests in providing and improving secure Services, to comply with legal obligations, and with consent where required.

6. How We Share Information

We share information only with:

  • Service providers under contract for hosting, storage, authentication, email, support, and security monitoring (e.g., cloud infrastructure such as AWS; database/auth such as Supabase; email/support tooling).
  • Institutional administrators when deployed for an institution, consistent with institutional policies and agreements.
  • Law, safety, and rights where required by law or to protect users, institutions, or our Services.
  • Business transfers in connection with a merger, acquisition, or reorganization.

We do not "sell" or "share" personal information as defined by CCPA/CPRA.

7. International Transfers

We may transfer information to countries where our service providers operate (e.g., the United States). Where required, we use appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses.

8. Cookies

We use strictly necessary cookies for login sessions, security, load balancing, and user preferences. If we add analytics or advertising cookies in the future, we will update this Notice and, where required, request your consent.

9. Security

We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards, including encryption in transit and at rest, role‑based access controls, multi‑factor authentication for administrative systems, and continuous logging/monitoring. No method is completely secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Data Retention

We retain personal information as long as needed to provide the Services and meet legal/contractual obligations. Institutional data is returned and deleted pursuant to contract; upon termination or verified request, we delete hosted copies within 30 days unless law requires longer retention.

11. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access, correction, deletion, and portability of your personal information.
  • Opt‑out of "sale/sharing" (not applicable—we don't sell/share personal information; if this changes we'll provide a mechanism).
  • Limit use of sensitive information (not used in ways requiring CPRA "limit" rights).
  • Appeals: If we deny a request, you may appeal by replying to our decision email.

Submit requests to pocketpalrevolution@gmail.com. If we process your information for an institution, we may direct your request to that institution.

12. Children's Privacy

The Services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

13. Changes to This Notice

We may update this Notice from time to time. We will post updates with a new "Effective date." If changes materially affect your rights, we will notify you and your institution as required.

14. Contact Us

Questions about this Notice or our privacy practices? Email pocketpalrevolution@gmail.com.
